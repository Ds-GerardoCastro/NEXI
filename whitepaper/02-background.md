## 2.5 Position of this work

The Nature Intelligence Project sits at the intersection of these literatures. NEXI does not duplicate any of them: it is not a biology survey (the source ingestion is purposive), not a pattern book (the curation discipline is stricter), and not a critique paper (the program is constructive). The contribution is the _methodology_ connecting comparative cognition to AI design with provenance and falsifiability preserved end-to-end.
