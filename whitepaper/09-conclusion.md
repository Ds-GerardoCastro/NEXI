# 9. Conclusion

## 9.1 Recap

The argument of this white paper rests on two converging critiques of modern AI: the architectural critique that LLM-based agentic systems lack the world models required for reliable planning, and the template critique that AI's biological inspiration descends from a single perceptron lineage and has barely engaged with the broader intelligence-design space documented across species. The two critiques converge on a single research direction: world models should be informed by the plurality of natural intelligence designs, not by scaling up one cortical template.

The methodology of Section 4 operationalises that direction: a pipeline that turns peer-reviewed comparative-cognition literature into engineering-ready architectural primitives, with provenance preserved, falsifiability required, and citation discipline enforced end-to-end. The framework of Section 5 organises the resulting primitives at two layers — patterns and collections — and recognises four distinct collection shapes that have emerged from the catalog so far: constellation, pipeline, composition (the three shapes that emerged from the first three collections), and isomorphism (the fourth shape, contributed by a subsequently-ingested vertebrate-action-selection source).

The case studies of Section 6 walk through the first three collections as worked examples; §6.6 summarises the fourth collection as an addendum. The catalog at version 1 contains sixteen patterns across five collections drawn from five peer-reviewed sources, with three patterns (`niche-specification`, `action-selection-as-common-substrate`, and `multi-modal-integration`) promoted to canonical — the first on convergent two-paper evidence, the second on cross-substrate triangulation across vertebrate-CBGTC, bacterial-molecular, and cnidarian-nerve-net implementations, and the third on cross-phyletic co-sourcing of a convergently-selected multimodal-integration centre. Four of the five collections have yielded distinct structural shapes (constellation, pipeline, composition, isomorphism); the fifth, substrate-independent-cognition, is newly seeded from a single source and is not yet assigned a distinct shape. The shape diversity is itself an empirical finding — nature does not assemble adaptive behaviour in one canonical way, and a catalog that recognises this can place architectural claims at the shape level, not only at the pattern level.

## 9.2 Contribution claim

Four contributions are argued for in this work.

A _named, structured critique_ of AI's narrow biological inspiration — the template pillar — articulated alongside the more familiar architectural critique. The two-pillar framing is itself the contribution; neither pillar alone suffices to motivate the research direction the program targets.

A _methodology_ for translating peer-reviewed comparative cognition into AI design primitives, with provenance and falsifiability preserved end-to-end and a deterministic citation-verification gate enforced before any external publication.

An _open catalog_ (NEXI) that puts the methodology into practice — sixteen patterns across five collections, organised at two layers, with disciplined curation and an explicit canonical-promotion criterion that has now been satisfied by three patterns (one via convergent-two-paper evidence, one via cross-substrate triangulation, one via cross-phyletic co-sourcing).

A _shape-diversity finding_ at the collection level: constellation, pipeline, composition, and isomorphism are four architecturally distinct ways the catalog has already documented adaptive behaviour assembling itself. The framework is open to additional shapes, and the empirical claim that shape diversity is real — strengthened by the fourth shape's clean absorption into the framework after the three-shape finding was articulated — is itself a research-relevant finding rather than an organisational convenience.

## 9.3 Position in the conversation

The work complements rather than displaces the world-model research direction; it challenges scaling-only framing, AGI-as-niche-abstracted-target, and pattern-bolt-on practices that ignore complementarity. It mines comparative cognition rather than imitating it. It inherits the pattern-catalog form from Alexander and the Gang-of-Four but commits to peer-reviewable provenance and per-pattern falsifiability that those traditions did not require. The white paper is the foundation; the catalog is the long arc; the program is open and ongoing.

## 9.4 Closing note

Intelligence as it actually exists in biological species takes many forms. Modern AI has explored a small slice of that design space. This work is an invitation — to AI engineers, to biologists, to philosophers of mind — to widen the slice deliberately, with the discipline that lets the resulting design library remain defensible, falsifiable, and useful.
