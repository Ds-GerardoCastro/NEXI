# References — Context-Bound Semantics

## Primary natural-system literature

- **Hagedoorn, K., Tschirren, N., ter Avest, E., Tyson, C., Snijders, L., Griffith, S. C., Naguib, M., & Loning, H. (2025).** _Communication Networks of Wild Zebra Finches (Taeniopygia castanotis)._ bioRxiv preprint, posted 2025-09-11. DOI: [10.1101/2025.09.11.675577](https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.09.11.675577). License CC-BY-NC-ND 4.0. _Unrefereed preprint; observational study._
  _Reports context-dependent candidate functions of zebra finch song (the authors suggest different roles in breeding colony vs. social hotspot). Cited here as a natural analog for context-conditional interpretation, not as empirical support for the ML hypothesis._

- **Snijders, L., & Naguib, M. (2017).** _Communication in animal social networks: a missing link?_ Advances in the Study of Behavior, 49, 297–359.
  _Treatment of context-dependent signal function in animal-communication ecology._

## Pragmatics and context-binding

- **Grice, H. P. (1975).** _Logic and conversation._ In: Cole, P. & Morgan, J. (Eds.), _Syntax and Semantics, Vol. 3: Speech Acts_. Academic Press.
  _Foundational treatment of conversational implicature — meaning beyond the literal signal._

- **Goodman, N. D., & Frank, M. C. (2016).** _Pragmatic language interpretation as probabilistic inference._ Trends in Cognitive Sciences, 20(11), 818–829.
  _Rational Speech Acts (RSA) framework — formalises pragmatic interpretation as conditional inference over speaker meaning given context._

## Computational analogs

- **Perez, E., Strub, F., de Vries, H., Dumoulin, V., & Courville, A. (2018).** _FiLM: Visual Reasoning with a General Conditioning Layer._ AAAI 2018.
  _Feature-wise Linear Modulation — the canonical mechanism for context-conditioning a decoder, adding only a small number of parameters._

- **Andreas, J., & Klein, D. (2016).** _Reasoning about Pragmatics with Neural Listeners and Speakers._ EMNLP 2016.
  _Computational instantiation of pragmatic reasoning in neural NLP._
