# References — Multi-Modal Integration

## Primary natural-system literature (single-source)

This is a **single-source** NEXI: one cross-phyletic anatomical source supports the integration commitment.

- **Roth, G. (2015).** _Convergent evolution of complex brains and high intelligence._ Philosophical Transactions of the Royal Society B, 370, 20150049. DOI: [10.1098/rstb.2015.0049](https://doi.org/10.1098/rstb.2015.0049).
  _Comparative synthesis documenting the convergent emergence of dedicated multimodal integration centres across five independent lineages — insect mushroom bodies, octopod vertical lobe, cichlid pallium, corvid/psittacid avian pallium, and mammalian isocortex. None are anatomical homologues, yet all share the commitment of integrating multiple sensory streams on a shared associative substrate ("multimodal centres … highly ordered associative networks"). The circuit figures, as reviewed by Roth (2015), read at the substrate level as expansion-and-convergence: the honeybee mushroom body has ~300,000 Kenyon cells receiving from ~800 projection neurons (an expansion; the ~300,000 count is given by Roth as a personal communication from Menzel, not a published primary count), and the octopus vertical lobe has ~26M interneurons converging on ~65,000 projection neurons (a convergence, which Roth reviews from primary sources — Young; Shomrat & Hochner). This is the pattern's single, cross-phyletic and anatomical, natural source._

With one natural leg, the NEXI is not multi-source and stands as a draft.

## Computational analogs

- **Radford, A., et al. (2021).** _Learning Transferable Visual Models from Natural Language Supervision (CLIP)._ ICML 2021.
  _Foundational cross-modal contrastive alignment of image + text into a shared latent space._

- **Jia, C., et al. (2021).** _Scaling Up Visual and Vision-Language Representation Learning with Noisy Text Supervision (ALIGN)._ ICML 2021.
  _Same architectural choice as CLIP at scale; demonstrates the joint-encoding approach generalises._

- **Girdhar, R., et al. (2023).** _ImageBind: One Embedding Space To Bind Them All._ CVPR 2023.
  _Extension to six modalities (image, text, audio, depth, thermal, IMU) via shared latent space, cross-modal contrastive alignment._

### Predictive joint-embedding (within-modality) — boundary, not analog

These are self-supervised _predictive_ methods within a single modality, not cross-modal contrastive alignment. Listed only to mark the boundary of what this NEXI covers.

- **Assran, M., et al. (2023).** _Self-Supervised Learning from Images with a Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture (I-JEPA)._ CVPR 2023.
  _Predicts in a joint latent space within the image modality; a within-modality predictive method, not a cross-modal analog._

- **Bardes, A., et al. (2024).** _V-JEPA: Latent Video Prediction for Visual Representation Learning._ arXiv preprint.
  _Video-domain within-modality predictive method; not a cross-modal contrastive analog._
