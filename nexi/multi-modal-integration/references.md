# References — Multi-Modal Integration

## Primary natural-system literature

- **Loning et al. (2026).** _Communication Networks of Wild Zebra Finches (Taeniopygia castanotis)._ bioRxiv preprint, posted 2026-04-27. License CC-BY-NC-ND 4.0.
  _Empirical demonstration that song-based and spatial co-occurrence networks mirror each other in zebra finch colonies — vocal and spatial channels carry convergent information._

- **Snijders, L., & Naguib, M. (2017).** _Communication in animal social networks: a missing link?_ Advances in the Study of Behavior, 49, 297–359.
  _Theoretical framework linking communication and movement networks; basis for treating cross-modal observation as fundamental rather than incidental._

## Computational analogs

- **Radford, A., et al. (2021).** _Learning Transferable Visual Models from Natural Language Supervision (CLIP)._ ICML 2021.
  _Foundational cross-modal contrastive alignment of image + text into a shared latent space._

- **Jia, C., et al. (2021).** _Scaling Up Visual and Vision-Language Representation Learning with Noisy Text Supervision (ALIGN)._ ICML 2021.
  _Same architectural choice as CLIP at scale; demonstrates the joint-encoding approach generalises._

- **Girdhar, R., et al. (2023).** _ImageBind: One Embedding Space To Bind Them All._ CVPR 2023.
  _Extension to six modalities (image, text, audio, depth, thermal, IMU) via shared latent space._

- **Assran, M., et al. (2023).** _Self-Supervised Learning from Images with a Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture (I-JEPA)._ CVPR 2023.
  _Predict in a joint latent space rather than per-modality; theoretically aligned with the spirit of this NEXI._

- **Bardes, A., et al. (2024).** _V-JEPA: Latent Video Prediction for Visual Representation Learning._ arXiv preprint.
  _Video-domain extension of JEPA; central to the broader Nature Intelligence Project's architectural argument._
